Welcome to Heart Track

Heart Track is a project aimed at providing real-time monitoring of heart rate and blood oxygen levels, offering easy access to crucial health data.

IoT Information

The Argon IoT Starter Kit from Particle is an IoT development kit built for creating reliable, connected devices with Wi-Fi capability. It includes an Argon microcontroller that supports Wi-Fi networking, making it ideal for prototyping IoT projects. With a powerful ARM Cortex M4 processor, built-in Bluetooth Low Energy (BLE), and a variety of sensors and components, this kit is particularly suitable for monitoring and data-gathering applications.
